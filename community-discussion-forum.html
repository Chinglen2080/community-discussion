Community Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines to maintain a welcoming, productive, and safe environment for everyone.

‚úÖ Community Standards

ü§ù Be Respectful

  • Treat all community members with respect and courtesy
  • Avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language
  • Respect different opinions and experience levels
  • Use constructive criticism rather than negative comments

üí¨ Stay On Topic

  • Keep discussions relevant to the forum category
  • Use appropriate titles that describe your topic
  • Don't hijack threads with unrelated topics
  • Search before posting to avoid duplicates

üîí Privacy & Security

  • Don't share personal information publicly
  • Report security vulnerabilities privately first
  • Respect others' privacy and confidentiality
  • Use secure channels for sensitive discussions

üìù Quality Content

  • Provide clear, detailed posts with proper context
  • Use proper formatting and grammar when possible
  • Include relevant technical details for support requests
  • Contribute constructive and helpful responses

‚ùå Prohibited Content

  • Spam, advertisements, or self-promotion without value
  • Harassment, threats, or abusive behavior
  • Illegal content or activities
  • Malicious software or security exploits
  • Piracy or copyright infringement
  • Political discussions unrelated to privacy/security
  • Doxxing or sharing others' private information
  • Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior

‚öñÔ∏è Moderation Policy

1. Community Self-Moderation

Members are encouraged to help maintain quality by providing helpful feedback and reporting issues.

2. Moderator Review

Our volunteer moderators review reported content and take appropriate action based on guidelines.

3. Progressive Actions

  • Warning: First offense - friendly reminder of guidelines
  • Temporary Restriction: Repeated violations - limited posting ability
  • Suspension: Serious violations - temporary ban from community
  • Permanent Ban: Severe or repeated violations after warnings

4. Appeals Process

Contact community@enclave-messenger.com if you believe a moderation action was taken in error.
